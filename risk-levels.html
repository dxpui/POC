Risk levels

Using your findings from Step 2, tell us what level of risk you have assigned to each kind of primary priority content, each kind of priority content, and the two kinds of non-designated content identified by Ofcom.

This will help identify which recommended measures from the Codes of Practice you should implement on your service.

Make sure the risk levels you assign are as accurate as possible to get the best information for your specific service. If you don’t assess your risk levels accurately, this could mean that you have failed in your duty to complete a suitable and sufficient risk assessment, and this may result in enforcement action.
