Risk Profiles are the lists of different risk factors that represent a selection of service characteristics (such as user base, business models and functionalities) that our Register of Risks indicates are most strongly linked to a risk of one or more kinds of illegal harm.

The Register of Risks is a document setting out the findings of Ofcom's own risk assessment for illegal harms. It sets out detailed evidence on risk factors that we have used to inform the Risk Profiles.

For user-to-user services, functionalities are defined as including features that enable interaction between users. Functionalities for search services are defined as including features that enable users to search websites or databases, as well as features that make suggestions relating to users’ search requests. Relevant provisions in the Act – see section 233(2).
