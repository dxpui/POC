Checklist

  • You have consulted Ofcom’s Risk profiles and identified the risk factors your service has for each kind of illegal harm. If you have used this tool, the relevant risk factors were provided as a list based on your answers.
  • You have also considered the 3 general risk factors (user base demographics, business model, commercial profile) and how they relate to your business.
  • You have recorded any risk factors relevant to your service, and confirmed that your service has consulted Ofcom’s Risk profiles .
